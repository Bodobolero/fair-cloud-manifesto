# Paid per use services

This category provides examples of 
paid per use services that you pay for but that are free from commercials and do not sell your data or collect it for purposes other than optimizing the specific service or set of services that you subscribed to

##Blende, a pay per use, commercial free news aggregator

At https://blendle.com/ you can read individual articles from leading magazines and newspapers for a small fee.

It provides apps for the major mobile devices that are free from commercials.
